Vegan Cookies

Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Prep time:25 minutes

Cook time:10 minutes

Servings: 36

Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

1 cup almond butter

1 cup packed brown sugar

2/3 cup shortening

1/3 cup vegan margaine

1 overripe banana, mashed

2.5 cups all-purpose flour

1.5 teaspoons baking soda

1 teaspoon baking powder

.5 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon water, or as needed

2 ounces dark chocolate, chopped

.5 cup white sugar

36 whole blanched almonds, optional

Directions

Step One

Preheat over to 375 degree F, 190 degree C

Step Two

Mix almond butter, brown sugar, shortening, and margarin together in a bowl until creamy. Stir in mashed banana

Step Three

Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Stir into almond butter mixture. Stir in water if dough appears too thick. Fold in dark chocolate

Step Four

Pour white sugar onto a shallow dish. Form dough into 1-inch balls and roll in sugar. Arrange 2 inches apart on baking sheets; flatten with a fork. Top each cookie with an almond

Step Five

Back in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely

Cooking Notes:

To blanch almonds: Place almonds in boiling water for 30 seconds, then remove with a slotted spoon and squeeze them out of their skins
